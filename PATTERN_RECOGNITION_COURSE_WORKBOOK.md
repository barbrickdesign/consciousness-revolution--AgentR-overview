### 1.2 THE 10 CORE MANIPULATION PATTERNS

**The 10 Patterns:**

1. **Artificial Urgency** - "Act now or lose forever"
2. **False Authority** - "Trust me, I'm an expert"
3. **Social Proof Fabrication** - "Everyone's doing it"
4. **Emotional Hijacking** - Using fear/anger to bypass logic
5. **Complexity Drowning** - Overwhelming with information
6. **Future Promise** - "It'll be amazing later" (never now)
7. **Victim Reversal** - Making manipulator appear victimized
8. **Partial Truth** - True facts arranged to create false conclusion
9. **Isolation** - Separating target from support system
10. **Gaslighting** - Making you doubt your own perception

### 3.3 NEGOTIATION PATTERN RECOGNITION

**Negotiation Manipulation Tactics:**

**Tactic 1: Anchoring**
- They state extreme position first
- Makes their real ask seem reasonable
- Defense: State your range first, or ignore anchor

**Tactic 2: Good Cop / Bad Cop**
- Two negotiators, one harsh, one friendly
- Friendly one "saves you" with bad deal
- Defense: Recognize the play, negotiate with decision maker only

**Tactic 3: Artificial Deadline**
- "Offer expires today"
- Creates urgency to prevent thinking
- Defense: Walk away, or call bluff with "Then I'll pass"

**Tactic 4: Nibbling**
- Agreement reached, then "one more small thing"
- Repeated until deal is terrible
- Defense: "That wasn't part of agreement, either renegotiate all terms or no"

**Tactic 5: Flinching**
- Physical recoil at your offer
- Makes you lower ask immediately
- Defense: Stay silent, wait for counter

### 4.1 MEDIA MANIPULATION PATTERNS

**How News Manipulates:**

**Pattern: Selective Reporting**
- Report 10% of story that fits narrative
- Omit context that would change interpretation
- Defense: Seek multiple sources, including opposition

**Pattern: Emotional Framing**
- Same facts, different emotional packaging
- "Freedom fighter" vs "Terrorist" (same person)
- Defense: Strip emotion, examine facts only

**Pattern: False Balance**
- Give equal time to unequal positions
- "Some say Earth is round, others say flat"
- Defense: Check credentials, evidence, consensus

**Pattern: Manufactured Consensus**
- "Experts agree" (which experts?)
- "Studies show" (which studies?)
- Defense: Demand specific citations
