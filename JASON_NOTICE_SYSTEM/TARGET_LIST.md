## DELIVERY METHOD OPTIONS

### Option A: USPS Certified Mail (Recommended for Legal Record)
- **Cost:** ~$4-7 per letter domestic, $15-25 international
- **Tracking:** Full chain of custody
- **Return Receipt:** Green card proves delivery
- **Time:** 3-5 days domestic, 2-4 weeks international

### Option B: Registered Mail (Maximum Security)
- **Cost:** ~$15-20 domestic, $30+ international
- **Tracking:** Signed at every step
- **Insurance:** Available
- **Time:** Slower but ironclad proof

### Option C: International Registered (For Vatican)
- **Cost:** ~$30-50
- **Tracking:** International tracking number
- **Proof:** Delivery confirmation

### Option D: Email + Physical (Belt & Suspenders)
- Send email first as notice
- Follow with certified mail
- Documents both methods
