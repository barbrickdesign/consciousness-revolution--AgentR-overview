Authority of the Speaker

Jason Evdoxiadis Rootwalker acts as Chief Na Ha Dayax Gixpaxloats, Speaker for the Gitxoon, and Chief of Chiefs of the Tsimshian Nation. This authority is exercised under the Gitxoon's full authority to do all Speaker deems necessary to insure the survival of the next seven generations.

Key Legal Concepts

Void Ab Initio

Latin for "void from the beginning." When something is void ab initio, it is treated as though it never existed. All derivative claims from a void instrument are therefore also void.

Fraud from Origin

When the foundational instrument or authority was fraudulently constituted at inception, all subsequent claims derived from it are tainted by the original fraud.

Acquiescence by Silence

A legal doctrine whereby silence or failure to rebut a claim within a reasonable time constitutes admission and agreement with the claim.

Demand for Cure

A formal notice requiring the recipient to remedy a default or breach within a specified time period. Failure to cure results in default.

Referenced Principles

Natural Law

  • Conscience Rights

    No authority can compel obedience contrary to conscience. This is a fundamental principle of natural law that predates and supersedes positive law.

  • Delegation of Authority

    Authority cannot be delegated that was never possessed. If the original grant was fraudulent, all subsequent delegations are void.

Equity Principles

  • He Who Seeks Equity Must Do Equity

    One cannot claim equitable relief while engaging in inequitable conduct.

  • Clean Hands Doctrine

    A party seeking equitable relief must not have acted inequitably in relation to the matter at issue.

  • Unjust Enrichment

    It is inequitable for one party to be enriched at the expense of another without just cause.

The Demand Structure

Four-Part Demand for Cure

  • 1. Produce Authority

    Produce the original, unaltered authority granting instruments.

  • 2. Demonstrate Standing

    Demonstrate lawful standing and jurisdiction.

  • 3. Disclose Assets

    Disclose all assets, trusts, accounts, and properties derived from the fraudulent seed.

  • 4. Cease Operations

    Commence immediate cessation of claims, collections, coercions, and representations.

Process

Delivery Requirements

  • Certified Mail / Registered Delivery

    All notices should be sent via certified mail with return receipt requested, or equivalent registered delivery service with tracking.

  • 30-Day Cure Period

    Recipients have 30 days from date of service to respond and cure. Failure to do so constitutes final default.

  • Documentation

    All delivery receipts, responses, and correspondence must be preserved for the record.
