## REVIEW ACQUISITION STRATEGY

**Goal: 50+ reviews in first 90 days**

### Strategies:

1. **Advance Review Copies (ARC)**
   - Send to 50+ beta readers via BookFunnel or BookSprout
   - Include letter requesting honest review
   - Target: 20-30 reviews on launch day

2. **Personal Network**
   - Email friends, colleagues, past clients
   - Offer free copy in exchange for honest review
   - Don't ask for "positive" review (violates Amazon TOS)

3. **Book Review Bloggers**
   - Identify 50+ psychology/self-help book bloggers
   - Send personalized pitch with review copy offer
   - Target: 5-10 blog reviews + Amazon cross-posts

4. **Goodreads Giveaway**
   - List book for giveaway (eBook or paperback)
   - Attract readers, build wishlist adds
   - Winners often review

5. **NetGalley**
   - Professional review service for librarians, bloggers, media
   - Cost: $450 for 6-month listing
   - Higher quality reviews, library sales potential

6. **Social Proof in Book**
   - Include CTA at end: "If this book helped you, please leave a review"
   - Link to Amazon review page
   - Explain how reviews help other readers find book
