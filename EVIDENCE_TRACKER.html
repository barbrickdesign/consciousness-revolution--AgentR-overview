Evidence Best Practices

Strong Evidence Characteristics

  • Documented with dates and sources
  • Contemporaneous (created at the time of events)
  • From independent/neutral sources
  • Corroborated by multiple sources
  • Original documents, not copies when possible

Common Evidence Types by Dispute

  • Employment: Emails, performance reviews, witness statements, policies
  • Family/Custody: Communications, photos, school records, medical records
  • Financial: Bank statements, contracts, invoices, receipts
  • Medical: Treatment records, bills, second opinions
  • Property: Photos, appraisals, contracts, permits

Organizing Tips

  • Keep a timeline of events with corresponding evidence
  • Note what each piece of evidence proves
  • Identify gaps - what evidence do you still need?
  • Keep originals safe, work with copies
  • Back up digital evidence in multiple locations
